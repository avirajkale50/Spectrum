    What is Spectrum!!!

    In Ac. Yr. 2018-19; AS&H Department successfully organized " SPECTRUM" an annual technical symposium of AS&H. This symposium was initiated in 2008 and successively 12 years we conducted it with a grand success. Due to pandemic in 2019-20 it was not conducted but in 2020-21 we conducted this event in online platform with huge number of participants.The main objective to provide a platform exclusively for AS&H engineering students to express and apply their innovative ideas and hidden talents in the competitive technological world and to develop a global perspective as well as leadership skills among FY students.
